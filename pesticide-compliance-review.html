🔴 Critical — Greenhouse-prohibited or non-food-crop products

Stop applying immediately. These products are either prohibited in greenhouses or registered for ornamentals only.
ProductCropIssueRecommendation
Success Cuke (Spray) Label states: "Do not apply Success in greenhouses or other enclosed structures used for growing crops." Discontinue greenhouse use. Substitute Radiant SC (also spinosad chemistry, properly greenhouse-labeled).
Success Lettuce (Fogger) Same explicit greenhouse prohibition. Also exceeds 4–10 fl oz/A field max with 20 fl oz/A. Discontinue.
Avid 0.15 EC Cuke EPA Reg. 100-896 is registered for ornamentals only (Shadehouse, Greenhouse, Field-Grown Ornamentals, Foliage Plants, Christmas Trees, Other Woody Ornamentals). Not labeled for food crops. Stop applying to food crops. For abamectin on cucurbit/lettuce, use AgriMek SC (EPA Reg. 100-1351) which has food crop registration.
Avid 0.15 EC Lettuce Same — ornamental-only registration. Stop applying to food crops.
